Fast · Simple · Precise · Economical

The In-Situ Minerals Lab at Hefei University of Technology houses TELEDYNE CETAC AnalyteHE Excimer Laser and Agilent 7900 ICP-MS. This combination of laser ablation system and ICP-MS leverages high energy and spatial resolution, enabling high sensitivity in sample analysis. Our lab can provide in-depth chemical analysis and testing, aiding in the resolution of complex scientific issues related to geological processes, mineral resources, and the development of new materials from a microscopic perspective.

Mapping

Trace element mapping of rock-forming minerals, with higher data accuracy and greater spatial resolution.

Trace Elements

Accurate micro-area trace element analysis of ore-forming minerals, altered minerals, rock-forming minerals, materials, and fluid inclusions.

Dating

High-precision U-Pb geochronology of U-Th rich minerals in micro-regions (such as zircon, apatite, xenotime, monazite, titanite, etc.)

Analysis

Self-developed LIMS data analysis software supports Spot and Mapping.
